Why HTML Semantics Matter

Posted on July 29, 2025 by Abhinesh

What are Semantic Tags?

Semantic tags like <article>, <nav>, and <section> help search engines and screen readers understand your content.

Why Use Them?

Using semantic tags improves SEO, accessibility, and makes your code cleaner for other developers.
